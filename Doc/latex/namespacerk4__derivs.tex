\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
This module contains the derivatives of most short-\/term fluxes. 

Three subroutines live in this module. leaf\+\_\+derivs is a wrapper; leaftw\+\_\+derivs solves mostly the ground derivatives (soils and temporary surface water), and canopy\+\_\+derivs\+\_\+two solves derivatives at the cohort scale and the canopy air space at patch level. These routines are largely based on BRAMS LEAF-\/2/\+LEAF-\/3 model. \begin{DoxyAuthor}{Author}
Adapted from LEAF-\/2 by David Medvigy. Further updates to make it compatible with LEAF-\/3 and to conserve energy and water by Ryan Knox and Marcos Longo. 

Converted to module to eliminate interfaces by Marcos Longo 
\end{DoxyAuthor}
