\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Sub-\/routine and functions that flag whether cohorts are resolvable or not. 

These routines and functions define two logical flags (leaf\+\_\+resolvable and wood\+\_\+resolvable) that will decide whether cohorts can be solved in the water, energy, and CO2 budget. These flags ensure that the cohort is consistently skipped or resolved.
